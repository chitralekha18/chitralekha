Chitralekha Gupta

I am a Research Fellow at Human Language Technology Lab ECE Dept., National University of Singapore. I am working with Prof. Li Haizhou as my advisor.

My research interests are speech and music signal processing, singing voice evaluation and synthesis, music information retrieval, applications of ASR in music, and applications of music in education and health therapy. I am currently working on automatic evaluation techniques for singing voice signals, automatic lyrics alignment and recogntion techniques.
